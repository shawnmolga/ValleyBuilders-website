Windows and Doors

Your windows are not just a functional part of your home, but they also serve as a design focal point. Valley Builders can help you find the right windows as well as window treatments that will make your house the envy of the neighborhood. Having out of date windows not only compromises your home’s overall look, but it can also have an effect on your electric bill. Damaged or poorly installed windows can allow air to flow in and out of the house. Hot air leaking in the winter and cool air escaping in the summer make your airflow systems work harder than they need to. You can also be victim to unwanted critters entering your home as well as allergens, dirt and debris and mold and mildew causing moisture. Call Valley Builders today for a free estimate your new windows. We can help you find the right choice to flow with your style as well as get the most bang for your buck.
